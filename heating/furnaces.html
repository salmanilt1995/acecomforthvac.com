Common Furnace Problems

We Fix Every Furnace Issue

From minor repairs to complete system failures, our technicians carry the parts and expertise to resolve every common furnace problem efficiently.

🔥

No Heat

Igniter failure, gas supply issues, thermostat faults — we diagnose the root cause and fix it fast. Don't spend a cold night without heat.

🗣

Strange Noises

Banging, rattling, squealing, or clicking often indicate loose components, blower motor wear, or delayed ignition. We identify and resolve the source.

⛱

High Energy Bills

An inefficient or poorly maintained furnace works harder and costs more to run. A tune-up or upgrade can reduce heating costs by 20–30%.

♿

Short Cycling

Furnace turning on and off rapidly? This stresses the system and is usually caused by a dirty filter, overheating, or a faulty thermostat. Easy to fix when caught early.

💨

Carbon Monoxide Risk

A cracked heat exchanger is the most serious furnace issue — it can allow CO to enter your home. We inspect every system thoroughly and never ignore this safety risk.

🌡

Uneven Heating

Cold rooms despite the furnace running? Duct issues, airflow problems, or a undersized system may be the cause. We diagnose and solve distribution issues properly.
