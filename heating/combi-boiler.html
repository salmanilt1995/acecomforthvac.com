All-in-One Solution

One Unit for Space Heating and Hot Water — Nothing Else Needed

A combination boiler is a single, wall-mounted unit that provides both central heating and domestic hot water simultaneously, with no separate hot water storage tank required. Water is heated directly from the cold mains as it is needed, giving you instant hot water while also powering your radiators, radiant floors, or fan coils.

Combi boilers are exceptionally popular in Vancouver condos, townhomes, and any home where mechanical room space is limited. They eliminate the square footage consumed by a traditional hot water tank while delivering 95%+ efficiency and qualifying for FortisBC rebates.

AceComfort installs and services combi boilers from Navien, IBC, Bosch, Baxi, and Viessmann. We handle all gas line and plumbing work, integrate the unit with your existing hydronic distribution system, and register the warranty on your behalf.
