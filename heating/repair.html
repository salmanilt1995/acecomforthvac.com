Common Problems

Heating Problems We Fix Every Day

🔥

No Heat at All

If your furnace or boiler isn't producing heat, it could be an ignition failure, gas valve issue, blown control board, or thermostat problem. We diagnose fast and get heat restored the same visit in most cases.

🔊

Banging, Squealing & Rattling

Strange noises from your heating system are never normal. Banging on startup often signals delayed ignition — a safety hazard. Squealing points to worn bearings. We identify the source and fix it before it becomes a larger failure.

🌡

Uneven Heat / Cold Rooms

Some rooms cold while others are warm? The issue could be airflow restrictions, a failing zone valve, duct problems, or an undersized system working too hard. We trace the cause and restore even comfort throughout your home.

⛏

Pilot Light & Ignition Issues

A pilot that won't stay lit, a hot surface igniter that won't spark, or a burner that cycles on and off are all repairable issues — usually on the first visit. Don't let ignition issues linger; they can lead to complete system failure.

📈

Rising Energy Bills

A sudden spike in your gas or electric bill without any change in usage often points to a heating system running inefficiently — dirty burners, failing heat exchanger, or a thermostat that's lost calibration. We tune it back to peak performance.

💧

Leaks & Water Around Boiler

Water pooling around a boiler or water heater requires immediate attention. Whether it's a failing pressure relief valve, a corroded fitting, or an internal crack, we locate the leak and repair it safely before water damage sets in.
