Annual Heating Service Done Right Ś Not Just a Checkbox

🤖 AI-Powered Service Software

A lot of HVAC companies do a "tune-up" in 20 minutes and hand you a bill. That's not how we work. Our licensed heating technicians use the most advanced AI-powered service software available Ś every single service call follows a structured digital checklist so nothing gets missed, ever. The software guides our technicians through every step, flags anything outside of normal operating range, and generates a complete record of the work performed.

When we service your furnace, boiler, or heating system in Vancouver, here's what's actually included:

  • ✓Full Safety Inspection Ś We verify heat exchanger integrity, check for carbon monoxide risks, test all safety controls and limit switches, and inspect the flue and venting system.
  • ✓Combustion Analysis Ś We test combustion to confirm your system is not over-fired or under-fired and is not burning excessive gas. This protects your equipment and keeps your energy bills in check.
  • ✓Deep Cleaning Ś Burners, heat exchanger, blower wheel, flame sensor, and igniter are cleaned thoroughly. A dirty system works harder, costs more to run, and fails sooner.
  • ✓Visual Inspection of All Components Ś We inspect every part of the system from top to bottom to catch small issues before they become expensive problems.
  • ✓Efficiency Verification Ś We confirm your system is operating at manufacturer specifications so it heats your home the way it was designed to Ś no wasted energy.
  • ✓Detailed Invoice & Warranty Certificate Ś You receive a complete written report of everything done, the condition of each component, and a warranty certificate for the service. No vague invoices.

Our software ensures every technician follows the same thorough process on every visit Ś so you always get the same high standard of service, regardless of which technician comes to your door. This is how we deliver top-notch heating service across Vancouver and the entire Lower Mainland.

Signs You Need Heating Service

  • 🔥 Furnace or boiler not producing heat
  • 🔥 Uneven temperatures room to room
  • 🔥 Strange noises Ś banging, clicking, squealing
  • 🔥 Rising energy bills with no change in usage
  • 🔥 Pilot light issues or system won't ignite
  • 🔥 Thermostat not responding correctly
  • 🔥 System is 12+ years old
  • 🔥 Yellow or flickering burner flame
