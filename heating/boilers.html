Common Boiler Issues

Boiler Problems We Fix Every Day

⛈

No Heat or Hot Water

Pilot light issues, failed ignition, gas supply problems, or a faulty zone valve. We diagnose and fix the root cause quickly.

💧

Boiler Leaking

A leaking boiler is urgent. Pressure relief valves, corroded fittings, or a cracked heat exchanger — we stop the leak and address the underlying cause.

🗣

Banging or Kettling Sounds

Limescale buildup on the heat exchanger causes "kettling" — a rumbling, banging sound. A power flush and descaling service resolves this efficiently.

♿

Pressure Issues

Low or high boiler pressure can cause lockouts and reduce efficiency. We restore correct pressure and identify why it's fluctuating.

🌡

Uneven Heating

Cold radiators or radiant zones that won't heat up are often caused by airlock, zone valve failure, or a failing circulator pump. We isolate and fix the issue.

⛱

High Energy Bills

An inefficient boiler running below its rated efficiency wastes fuel every day. Annual servicing and potential upgrades can deliver significant savings.
