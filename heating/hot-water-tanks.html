Signs Your Tank Needs Attention

  • Running out of hot water faster than usual
  • Rust or discolouration in your hot water
  • Visible tank leaking or pooling water
  • Loud popping, rumbling, or banging sounds
  • Tank is 10 or more years old
  • Noticeably rising gas or hydro bills
  • Water that smells like rotten eggs

💧 Tank Leaking? Don't Wait.

A leaking hot water tank can fail completely within hours and cause serious water damage. Call us now for same-day service.

Your Options

Storage Tank vs. Tankless vs. Heat Pump Water Heater

Not sure which type of hot water system is right for your home? Here is a clear comparison to help you decide.

💧

Storage Tank

The familiar, reliable choice. Stores 40–80 gallons of hot water ready to use. Lower upfront cost, simple installation.

  • Lowest upfront cost
  • Simple and proven technology
  • Fast replacement — same day
  • Gas and electric models
⚡
Popular Upgrade

Tankless (On-Demand)

Heats water instantly as needed. No standby heat loss. Endless hot water and up to 40% energy savings.

  • Endless hot water on demand
  • Saves up to 40% on energy
  • Space-saving wall-mount design
  • FortisBC rebates available
♨

Heat Pump Water Heater

Uses heat pump technology to heat water with dramatically lower electricity consumption — up to 70% more efficient.

  • Up to 70% more efficient
  • BC Hydro rebates available
  • Also dehumidifies the space
  • Qualifies for Greener Homes Grant
