New Heating System — Sized Right, Installed Right

The biggest mistake homeowners make when replacing a heating system is choosing equipment based on price alone — without proper sizing. An oversized furnace short-cycles, creating temperature swings and excessive wear. An undersized one runs constantly without ever achieving comfort. AceComfort performs a proper heat load calculation for every installation to ensure you get exactly the right equipment for your home.

From there, we handle everything: equipment sourcing, permits, gas line work, venting, thermostat installation, and commissioning. We're certified installers for Carrier, Lennox, Napoleon, Navien, Bosch, and more — meaning you get factory training, genuine parts, and manufacturer warranty support.

We also identify every rebate you qualify for before the job begins, handle the paperwork, and make sure the installation meets all requirements for your rebate to be approved.
