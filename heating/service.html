Preventive Maintenance

Keep Your Heating System Running Safely and Efficiently All Year

The best time to discover a problem with your heating system is before it leaves you in the cold. An annual heating tune-up from AceComfort takes about 90 minutes — and it's the single most effective thing you can do to prevent unexpected breakdowns, keep energy bills under control, and maintain your manufacturer's warranty.

Our technicians follow a comprehensive 21-point inspection checklist that covers every critical component of your system. We clean what needs cleaning, adjust what needs adjusting, and flag anything that needs attention — all documented in a written service report you can keep for your records and warranty files.

Heating maintenance is also strongly recommended — and in some cases required — by BC gas safety regulations and by most manufacturer warranties. Skipping annual service risks voiding coverage that could save you thousands.

Why It Matters

What Regular Heating Service Actually Does for You

🔥

Carbon Monoxide Safety

A cracked heat exchanger can leak carbon monoxide into your living space — a colourless, odourless, potentially deadly gas. Our inspection includes a thorough heat exchanger check and CO measurement. This alone makes annual service worth every dollar.

📈

Lower Energy Bills

A dirty burner, restricted airflow, or out-of-calibration controls can reduce your heating system's efficiency by 20 — 30%. A clean, well-tuned system runs less frequently and uses less gas or electricity to achieve the same result — saving real money every month.

📅

Fewer Emergency Calls

The vast majority of heating breakdowns are preceded by warning signs a trained technician can spot during a routine visit — before the failure happens. Homeowners who maintain their systems regularly experience dramatically fewer no-heat emergencies during winter.
