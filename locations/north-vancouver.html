North Shore, BC

North Shore HVAC Specialists

North and West Vancouver homes face unique heating challenges. Higher elevations, heavy rainfall, and colder temperatures than the rest of Metro Vancouver mean your HVAC system works harder. Many North Shore homes also have radiant heating and boilers instead of forced air — our technicians are experts in both.

We serve all North Shore areas: Lonsdale, Lynn Valley, Deep Cove, Edgemont, Canyon Heights, Lions Gate, Dundarave, Ambleside, Caulfeild, and Horseshoe Bay.

Cold-Climate Heat Pumps for the North Shore

Standard heat pumps struggle below 0°C. We install cold-climate models from Mitsubishi, Daikin, and Bosch that maintain full efficiency down to -25°C — ideal for North Vancouver winters at elevation.
