Residential Duct Cleaning

The Most Thorough Duct Cleaning in Metro Vancouver

AceComfort uses a professional push-and-pull system — the most effective method available for residential duct cleaning. High-pressure air at 185+ PSI is pushed through every branch to dislodge buildup, while our advanced portable truck-mounted unit pulls everything out with over 5,000 CFM of suction power. Nothing is pushed around — it's all extracted.

Every job includes before-and-after photos taken inside your ducts so you can see exactly what was removed and confirm the work is done right. We don't just clean and leave — we show you the proof.

And since our team is already at your home with all equipment set up, it makes perfect sense to also clean your bathroom exhaust fans and dryer vent at the same time. It saves you time, saves you money, and ensures your entire home ventilation system is fully cleaned in one visit.

What We Clean

  • All supply and return duct branches
  • Main trunk lines (supply and return)
  • All registers and grilles (removed and cleaned)
  • Air handler unit and blower compartment
  • Bathroom exhaust fans
  • Dryer vent (full length)
  • Before-and-after photo documentation included
  • Optional antimicrobial fogging treatment

Smart Bundling — Save Time & Money

While our team is at your home with all equipment set up, we also clean your bathroom fans and dryer vent for the best results. Bundling saves you a separate service call and keeps your entire ventilation system clean in one visit.
