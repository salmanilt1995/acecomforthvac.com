Why It Matters

What's Living in Your Air Ducts Right Now?

Over time, your home or building's duct system accumulates a remarkable amount of material: pet dander, pollen, mold spores, bacteria, dust mite debris, construction dust, and in older homes, even pest remnants. Every time your HVAC system runs, it circulates this material through every room in the building.

The EPA estimates that indoor air can be 2–5 times more polluted than outdoor air. For allergy sufferers, asthma patients, young children, and the elderly, clean ducts aren't just a comfort issue — they're a health issue.

AceComfort uses professional-grade truck-mounted vacuum systems and rotary brush equipment to thoroughly clean your entire duct system — not just the vents you can see.

Our Process

How We Clean Your Air Ducts

We don't just stick a vacuum at the vent opening. Our process thoroughly cleans your entire duct system from the inside out.

1

Inspection

We begin with a visual inspection of your duct system and air handler to assess contamination levels and identify any damage.

2

Negative Pressure

We connect our truck-mounted vacuum system to create negative pressure in the duct system, preventing dust from escaping into your home.

3

Agitation & Removal

Rotary brushes and compressed air whips dislodge debris from duct walls while our vacuum continuously pulls everything out.

✓

Final Inspection

We inspect the completed work and can provide optional antimicrobial treatment to inhibit mold and bacterial growth in your duct system.
