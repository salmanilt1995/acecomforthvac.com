Commercial Duct Cleaning

The Most Thorough Commercial Duct Cleaning in Metro Vancouver

AceComfort uses a professional push-and-pull system — the most effective method available for commercial duct cleaning. High-pressure air at 185+ PSI is pushed through every branch and trunk line to dislodge buildup, while our advanced portable truck-mounted unit extracts everything with over 5,000 CFM of suction power. Nothing is redistributed — it's fully removed.

Every commercial job includes before-and-after photos taken inside your ducts so building owners, property managers, and strata corporations have complete documentation confirming the work is done correctly. Our records satisfy insurance requirements, health authority inspections, and building certification programs.

While our team is on site with all equipment set up, we also clean bathroom exhaust fans, kitchen exhaust vents, and dryer vents — saving you the cost of a separate service call and ensuring your entire building ventilation system is cleaned in one visit.

We work around your business hours, including evenings and weekends, to minimize disruption to your operations or tenants.
