Central AC

Whole-Home Cooling the Right Way

Central air conditioning uses your existing forced-air ductwork to deliver cool, dehumidified air to every room in your home through the same vents that heat you in winter. It connects to your furnace blower and provides even, consistent comfort across your entire home — all from a single outdoor unit and thermostat.

AceComfort installs systems from Carrier, Lennox, Trane, Napoleon, and other leading manufacturers. We perform a proper Manual J load calculation to size your system correctly — a critical step that many contractors skip. An oversized system short-cycles without removing humidity, leaving your home feeling cool but clammy. An undersized system runs constantly and cannot keep up on the hottest days.

Spring is the ideal time to install. Book early to get ahead of the summer rush.
