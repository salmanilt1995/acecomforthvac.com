Common AC Problems

AC Repair Issues We Fix Every Day

❄

AC Blowing Warm Air

If your system runs but doesn't cool, you may have a refrigerant leak, a failing compressor, a frozen evaporator coil, or a dirty condenser. We'll diagnose the exact cause and restore proper cooling quickly.

💧

Refrigerant Leaks

Low refrigerant causes poor cooling performance and can damage your compressor over time. We locate the leak, repair it, and recharge to factory specifications — not just a top-up that masks the problem.

🔊

Noisy Operation

Grinding, squealing, rattling, or banging from your AC unit points to mechanical issues — worn bearings, loose components, or compressor problems. We identify the source before it becomes a full system failure.

📈

Short Cycling

A system that turns on and off repeatedly every few minutes is short cycling — usually caused by an oversized system, a refrigerant issue, or a failing component. It wastes energy and creates uneven cooling. We find and fix the root cause.

💧

Water Leaks & Ice Buildup

Water dripping from your indoor unit or ice forming on the coils indicates airflow restriction or refrigerant problems. Left unaddressed, this causes water damage and coil failure. We clear the problem and prevent recurrence.

⚡

Electrical & Control Failures

Tripped breakers, faulty capacitors, failed contactors, and control board issues are common AC electrical problems. Our technicians are fully trained in AC electrical systems and carry common electrical parts on every van.
