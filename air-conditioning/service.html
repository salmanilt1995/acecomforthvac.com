Annual AC Maintenance

Keep Your AC Running at Peak Performance All Summer

Vancouver summers are unpredictable — but your AC shouldn't be. An annual spring tune-up ensures your cooling system is clean, fully charged, and operating efficiently before the heat arrives. Our AC maintenance visit covers a thorough inspection and cleaning of every major component — from the condenser coils and refrigerant levels to electrical connections and thermostat calibration. Why It Matters

The Real Value of Annual AC Maintenance

❄

Prevents Summer Breakdowns

Most AC failures are preceded by signs a trained technician can catch during a routine visit. A small investment in spring maintenance regularly prevents the expensive, inconvenient, and uncomfortable experience of an AC failure in July or August.

📈

Maintains Efficiency

Dirty coils and low refrigerant can reduce your system's cooling capacity by 20–30% while increasing electricity consumption. A clean, properly charged system delivers the cooling you're paying for — at the energy cost you expect.

📅

Extends System Life

Systems that receive annual maintenance consistently outlast neglected systems by 3–5 years. Given the cost of AC replacement, a $150–$200 annual tune-up pays for itself many times over across the life of the equipment.

The most important thing you can do is change or clean your air filter every 1–3 months — a dirty filter is the single leading cause of AC problems, including frozen coils and compressor damage. Keep the area around your outdoor condenser unit clear of debris and vegetation. Don't cover or block supply registers. And if you notice unusual sounds, odours, or reduced cooling, call us before the problem progresses.
