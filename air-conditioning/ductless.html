Ductless Mini-Split

Cool Any Room Without Touching Your Walls or Ceilings

Ductless mini-split systems are the most flexible air conditioning solution available. A compact outdoor unit connects to one or more sleek indoor units through a small hole in the wall — no ducts, no major renovation, no disruption. Installation typically takes one day.

Each indoor unit operates independently, giving you precise zone control. One bedroom can be cool while another stays warm. Family members with different preferences finally stop arguing about the thermostat. AceComfort installs systems from Mitsubishi Electric, Daikin, Carrier, and LG — brands known for whisper-quiet operation, reliability, and energy efficiency.

Many ductless models are also heat pumps — meaning you get both cooling in summer and efficient heating in winter from the same unit. These dual-purpose systems qualify for significant BC and federal rebates. Learn about ductless heat pumps →
