About AceComfort Heating & Air Conditioning

We started as a small team with a big belief: that every homeowner deserves honest, expert HVAC service without the stress, the runaround, or the inflated invoices. That belief still drives everything we do today.

Metro Vancouver's Trusted HVAC Team
Our Story

Built on Integrity. Grown on Referrals.

AceComfort was founded right here in Metro Vancouver with a straightforward mission — to be the HVAC company that homeowners actually feel good about calling. Too many people have been burned by contractors who overcharge, over-promise, and underdeliver. We set out to change that.

What started as a small operation serving a handful of Vancouver neighbourhoods has grown into one of the most trusted HVAC companies in the Lower Mainland — not through aggressive advertising, but through word of mouth from customers who couldn't believe how smooth the experience was.

Today, our team of certified technicians serves thousands of homes and businesses from North Vancouver to Abbotsford. We're still the same company we always were — local, honest, and genuinely invested in the communities we live and work in.

Ahead of the Curve in Customer Experience

We believe the best HVAC company isn't just defined by technical expertise — it's defined by how smoothly and thoughtfully every customer interaction is handled. That's why AceComfort has integrated SimplePrime CRM, an advanced AI-powered platform, into the heart of our operations.

SimplePrime allows us to manage your service history intelligently, communicate proactively, and ensure the right technician — with the right parts — is dispatched to your home every time. From booking confirmation to post-service follow-up, you're never left wondering what's happening or when.

We're proud to be among the first HVAC companies in Metro Vancouver to embrace this level of operational technology — because you deserve more than a technician who shows up. You deserve a team that's organized, informed, and committed to a seamless experience from start to finish.
