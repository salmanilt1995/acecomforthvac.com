Why Heat Pumps?

Heat & Cool Your Home With One Incredibly Efficient System

A heat pump doesn't generate heat — it moves it. In winter, it pulls warmth from the outdoor air and transfers it inside. In summer, it works in reverse as an air conditioner. The result is a single system that handles both heating and cooling at up to 300% efficiency.

For Vancouver homeowners, heat pumps are particularly smart. Our mild winters mean heat pumps can run efficiently year-round, even on cold days. And with BC's clean electricity grid, switching from gas to electric heat pumps significantly reduces your carbon footprint — and qualifies you for some of the best rebates in North America.

The Right Heat Pump for Every Home

Not all heat pumps are the same. The best choice depends on your home's size, existing ductwork, heating zones, and budget. We help you choose wisely.

Central Heat Pumps

Whole-home heating and cooling using your existing duct system. The most cost-effective upgrade for homes already equipped with forced-air distribution.

  • Uses existing ductwork
  • Heats & cools entire home
  • Replaces furnace + AC
  • High-efficiency models available

Ductless Heat Pumps

Flexible, room-by-room comfort control with no ductwork required. Mitsubishi and Daikin mini-splits are ideal for older homes, additions, or zone-control upgrades.
