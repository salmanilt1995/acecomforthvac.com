Most Popular Upgrade

Heat and Cool Your Home Without a Single Duct

A ductless heat pump is a single system that heats your home in winter and cools it in summer — with no ductwork required. A compact outdoor unit connects through a small opening in the wall to sleek indoor units that deliver quiet, targeted comfort exactly where you want it.

For Vancouver homes without existing ductwork, for condos and strata properties, for additions and renovations, and for anyone replacing electric baseboard heat — ductless is the clear, financially compelling choice. BC Hydro offers rebates specifically for homeowners replacing baseboard heating with a heat pump, making this one of the highest-rebate upgrades available in BC today.

AceComfort is a certified installer for Mitsubishi Electric and Daikin — widely regarded as the gold standard for cold-climate performance. Our installations are completed in one day and we handle every aspect of your BC Hydro, CleanBC, and Canada Greener Homes rebate applications.
