Annual Heat Pump Maintenance

Protect Your Investment With Regular Heat Pump Servicing

A heat pump is one of the most significant investments in your home — and unlike a furnace that only runs in winter, your heat pump works year-round in Vancouver's climate. Annual maintenance keeps it running at the 300%+ efficiency it was designed for, rather than the degraded performance that builds up gradually through dirty coils, low refrigerant, and worn components.

Our heat pump maintenance visits cover both heating and cooling performance — we test the system in both modes, clean the indoor and outdoor coils, verify refrigerant levels, check all electrical components, and clean the filters. You'll leave with a written service report and confidence going into any season.

For strata buildings and property managers with water source heat pump systems, we offer building-wide maintenance contracts with documented service records and priority scheduling.

Why It Matters

The Value of Annual Heat Pump Maintenance

📈

Maintains Peak Efficiency

Dirty coils reduce heat transfer efficiency significantly. A heat pump with fouled coils may consume 20–30% more electricity to deliver the same comfort — eroding the energy savings that made it worth buying in the first place.

♨

Prevents Compressor Failure

The compressor is the most expensive component in your heat pump. Low refrigerant, overheating from dirty coils, and electrical voltage irregularities are the leading causes of premature compressor failure — all of which are caught and addressed during annual service.

🆗

Protects Warranty Coverage

Most heat pump manufacturers require documented annual maintenance to maintain full warranty coverage. AceComfort provides detailed service records that satisfy warranty requirements for Mitsubishi, Daikin, Carrier, and other major brands.
