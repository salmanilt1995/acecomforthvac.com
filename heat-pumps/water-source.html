Premium Efficiency for Multi-Unit Buildings and Commercial Properties

Water source heat pumps (WSHPs) use a shared building water loop to exchange heat, providing highly efficient heating and cooling to individual suites or zones. Unlike air-source systems that depend on outdoor air temperature, water loop systems maintain a relatively constant source temperature year-round — which translates to consistently high efficiency and reliable operation regardless of outdoor weather conditions.

WSHPs are the standard HVAC solution in Metro Vancouver's strata condominiums, high-rise towers, hotels, and commercial office buildings. Each unit contains a self-contained heat pump that connects to the building's central water loop, with individual controls for each suite.

AceComfort provides complete water source heat pump services for property managers, strata corporations, and commercial building owners — from individual unit replacement and emergency repairs to full building maintenance contracts and water loop system servicing.
