Central Heat Pumps

One System. Year-Round Comfort. Maximum Rebates.

A central (ducted) heat pump is the most complete home comfort upgrade available. Using your existing ductwork, it replaces both your furnace and central air conditioner with a single, highly efficient unit that heats in winter and cools in summer — at efficiencies up to three times that of electric resistance heating.

For Vancouver homeowners with existing forced-air systems, this is usually the most financially compelling upgrade available right now. Your existing ductwork handles the distribution, so the installation is largely straightforward. And with BC Hydro, CleanBC, and Canada Greener Homes programs, the rebates can be substantial — sometimes exceeding $10,000 combined.

AceComfort installs central heat pumps from Carrier, Lennox, Trane, and other leading manufacturers. We perform proper load calculations, assess your existing ductwork, and configure your system precisely for your home and climate zone.

  • BC Hydro Heat Pump Rebate: up to $6,000
  • Canada Greener Homes Grant: up to $6,500
  • CleanBC Better Homes: up to $6,000
